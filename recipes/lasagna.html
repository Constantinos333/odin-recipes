Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella for the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef in a skillet; drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
