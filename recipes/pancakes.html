Pancakes

Description

Fluffy and golden pancakes perfect for a delicious breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a skillet and pour batter in circles.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
