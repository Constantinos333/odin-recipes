Salad

Description

A refreshing and healthy garden salad with crisp veggies and a light dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash and chop vegetables.
  2. Toss greens, cucumber, and tomato in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Serve fresh.
